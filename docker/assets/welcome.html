Welcome
Welcome to the simplified Sming development environment. If you look on the left side you will see a workspace pane. Under workscpace/Sming/samples you will find all available examples that will get you started. If you want to compile your first project we recommend you to try the Basic_Blink project. To do this go to the terminal window and type in there: 
              cd $SMING_HOME/../samples/Basic_Blink
              make
Once the compilation has finished you can upload your program on your ESP8266 device. Make sure that the device is connect to your computer on /dev/ttyUSB0 and type: 
              make flash
